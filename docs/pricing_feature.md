# The Golden Rule (Do Not Break This)

> **Free answers “Did something change?”
> Pro answers “What should I do about it?”**

If you remember nothing else, remember this.

---

# ✅ What Stays FREE (Forever, No Guilt)

Free is not crippled. It is **complete for light users**.

### Free Tier = “Awareness Mode”

**Purpose:** Prove reliability and earn belief.

### Free Includes:

* **1 competitor**
* **1 monitored page** (pricing OR homepage)
* **Daily checks**
* **Email alerts**
* **Descriptive AI**

  * What changed
  * Plain explanation
* **Last 5 alerts visible**
* **Manual “Check now”** (1×/day)

### Why this works:

* Indie founders *usually* track only 1 rival
* They still get real value
* They trust you before paying
* No resentment, no dark patterns

### What Free NEVER Includes:

* No historical analysis
* No prioritization
* No “importance” scoring
* No interpretation depth
* No scale

Free is honest — not generous.

---

# 💰 What Becomes PRO (With Surgical Precision)

Pro is not “more stuff.”
Pro is **decision leverage**.

---

## 🔥 Pro Tier = “Decision Mode”

### Pro Unlocks ONLY These Things:

### 1️⃣ Scale (The Obvious Upgrade)

* Unlimited competitors
* Unlimited pages per competitor
* Faster checks (hourly)

This is expected — not enough on its own.

---

### 2️⃣ Judgment (This Is the Core Value)

**Pro-only AI includes:**

* Likely intent (framed cautiously)
* Impact framing (“this may affect positioning / pricing”)
* Change classification:

  * Pricing move
  * Positioning shift
  * Feature expansion
  * Promotion test
* “Why this matters” section
* “What to consider doing” section

This is the **real paywall**.

Free = description
Pro = interpretation

---

### 3️⃣ Signal > Noise (Extremely Important)

Pro users get:

* “Meaningful change” filtering
* Fewer alerts, higher signal
* Grouped changes (avoid spam)
* “Ignore minor copy edits”

This alone converts serious users.

---

### 4️⃣ Memory (Underrated but Powerful)

Pro unlocks:

* Full alert history
* “Has this happened before?”
* Change timelines per competitor

Founders *love* memory.

---

### 5️⃣ Control (For People Who Rely on You)

Pro only:

* Custom check frequency
* Page section focus
* Alert sensitivity

This says: “This is now a tool you depend on.”

---

# 🚫 What NEVER Goes Behind Paywall (Critical Trust Rule)

If you gate these, users will hate you:

❌ Seeing that a change occurred
❌ Access to the alert email itself
❌ The basic diff
❌ The first meaningful alert

If someone gets surprised *after* paying, churn explodes.

# 🎯 Upgrade Trigger Moments (These Convert Without Pressure)

Show upgrade **only** when intent is proven.

Trigger Pro CTA when:

* User adds a 2nd competitor
* User clicks alert history
* User clicks “Why does this matter?”
* User manually checks multiple times

### Upgrade Copy (Use This)

> You’ve reached the free limit.
>
> Pro is for founders who rely on competitor moves to make decisions — not just stay informed.

This framing avoids guilt and hype.

If someone won’t pay $19 for this, they won’t be a good customer.
