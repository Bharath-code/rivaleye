*   **Buyer Profile**: SEMRush, G2, HubSpot, or a direct competitor looking to acquire your technology and "Oracle" data.

## 🛡️ Unit Economics & Margin Protection

Costs stay low because we scale sub-linearly with user growth:

1.  **Crawl Decay**: Frequent checks only happen on "High Volatility" pages. 80% of pages are static and cost <$0.01/month.
2.  **Shared RAG Cache**: Common competitor research (e.g., "What is Vercel's pricing?") is cached across the global Knowledge Base, reducing Firecrawl/AI spend per seat.
3.  **Self-Correction**: AI-generated interpretation happens once per change, not once per user view.
